Bernstein-Vazirani Quantum Algorithm - Completed

This program was a part of my research on the Bernstein-Vazirani Quantum Algorithm. The research argued that rather than using the commonly employed Deutsch algorithm to teach quantum computing, it's more beneficial to utilize the Bernstein-Vazirani Algorithm. This approach is less abstract, making it easier for undergraduate students to grasp.

Developed in collaboration with my esteemed research mentor, who is guiding me through the intricacies of quantum computing, this project is a key step in my journey to comprehend and contribute to this cutting-edge field.

  • Duration: Dec 2023 - Apr 2024
  • Role: Sole Developer
  • Technologies: Python, Replit
